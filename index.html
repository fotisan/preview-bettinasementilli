FAQ

A few common questions about art therapy.

Do we talk or just make art?

Both, depending on what helps you most. Some sessions are more verbal, others use imagery first. We choose the balance together, at a pace that feels safe.

Do I need to be good at art?

No. Art therapy is not about talent or performance. We use simple, accessible materials to help you notice feelings, patterns, and needs that may be hard to put into words.

Is this covered by insurance?

Cigna is accepted. If you have out-of-network benefits, I can provide a superbill so you can request reimbursement from your insurer.
