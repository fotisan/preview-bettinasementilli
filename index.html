Art Psychotherapist

Patience, Commitment, Growth

As a board-certified art psychotherapist, the most rewarding part of my work is the relief clients feel when progress becomes real. Some experiences are hard to say out loud. Sometimes we do not even know why we react the way we do. Art therapy offers a safe form of expression through images, helping you tell your story without pressure.

Art therapy plus talk therapy. We use both image and language, so you can process experiences in the way that fits you.

DBT-informed skills. Practical tools for emotion regulation, distress tolerance, and steadier relationships.

A grounded, respectful pace. The work can be challenging, but you stay in control of the speed and depth.
